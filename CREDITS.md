# Credits

## Drum samples

GrooveMate's acoustic drum sound is a curated subset of **MuldjordKit** by
**Lars Muldjord** ([drumgizmo.org](https://drumgizmo.org)), in the stereo
edition assembled by **Roberto Gordo Saez** for the
[FreePats project](https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Percussion/acoustic-drum-kit.html).

Licensed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
The full license text ships with the samples in
`Resources/DrumKits/MuldjordKit/LICENSE.txt`. Samples were re-organized and
renamed for GrooveMate's kit-manifest format; audio content is unmodified apart
from tail truncation at load time.
